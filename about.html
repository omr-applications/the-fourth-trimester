About us

Building villages for the fourth trimester

because mums need holding too

This group is for the days when motherhood feels beautiful, messy, overwhelming, tender, and completely new all at once.

A place to feel held, not judged

The Fourth Trimester is being built with the feel of a local, family-run group: warm welcomes, familiar faces, soft sensory play, and time for real conversation.

Every session is shaped around the idea that mums deserve care too. The baby matters deeply, and so does the person holding them.

  • Warm, friendly group setting
  • Professionally led topics and practical guidance
  • Baby-friendly space with room to feed, change, pause, and settle
  • A soft community feel that helps mums build real connections

You do not have to do this alone

The goal is simple: leave each session feeling a little more supported, a little more informed, and a little less alone.
