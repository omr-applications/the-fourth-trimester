FAQs

Helpful things to know

before you come along

A few gentle answers for the questions mums usually have before joining a new group.

Who is the programme for?

Mums with babies in the early months. The current launch guide is newborn to around 9 months, but final age guidance can be adjusted.

Do I need to attend all 10 sessions?

The programme is designed as a full 10-session journey, so booking the full programme is recommended.

Can I feed or change my baby during the session?

Yes. The group should feel baby-led and flexible. Feeding, changing, settling, and stepping out are all expected.

How do payments work?

Bookings will be taken through the connected booking provider. The recommended setup is Bookwhen connected to PayPal or Stripe.

Can I book for a group?

Yes. The booking page includes a group enquiry option so multiple places or private group enquiries can be handled clearly.
