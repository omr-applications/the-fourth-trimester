Built for the early days

Support for the baby, and for the person holding them

The Fourth Trimester is a small, welcoming programme for mums who want more than a class. It is a place to ask questions, learn a little, meet other mums, and feel looked after too.

What is included

A simple rhythm for support, learning, and belonging

1

Baby sensory

Soft, gentle play for little discoveries. Nothing loud or overwhelming, just simple ideas you can enjoy together.

2

Topical discussions

Friendly, professionally led chats about the things that actually come up in the early months.

3

Social mum time

Time to talk, listen, laugh, feed, settle, or just sit quietly with people who understand the season you are in.
